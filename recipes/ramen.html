Ramen Soup for Cold Winters!

Savor the Flavor: Mastering the Art of Homemade Ramen Noodles

Not only healthy, but also extremely delicious!

A bowl of Ramen is everything you need to make a cold day better!

Ingredients

  • 3 ½ cups vegetable broth
  • 1 (3.5 ounce) package ramen noodles with dried vegetables
  • 2 teaspoons soy sauce
  • ½ teaspoon chili oil
  • ½ teaspoon minced fresh ginger root
  • 1 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 2 green onions, sliced

How to prepare

  1. In a medium saucepan combine broth and noodles. Cover and bring to a boil over high heat.
  2. Stir to break up noodles.
  3. Reduce heat to medium and add soy sauce, chili oil and ginger. Simmer, uncovered, for 10 minutes.
  4. Stir in sesame oil and garnish with green onions.
